Python for Everybody

by Dr. Charles Severance


I am taking this course Python for Everybody by Dr. Charles Severance. Dr. Chuck is the Clinical Professor at the University of Michigan. Dr. Chuck is teaching this course for free.

It is amazing that this course is one of the most popular Python course in the world, with millions people enrolled. I think Dr. Chuck created this course with the smallest budget, he single handed the whole course, in his spare time, creating contents and teaching from home.

As the name suggests, this is a "for every body" course, hence it's not as challenging as other courses from Harvard, Stanford or MIT.

On a side note, Dr. Chuck has a series of free courses, that I intend to take after this course.
